Butter Noodles Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine Noodles or any other noodles 16 ounce dry
  • Butter Salted or Unsalted, 1 cup
  • Parmesan 1 cup
  • Salt and Black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Fill pot with salted water and bring to a boil.
  3. Stir in fettuccine, bring back to a boil, and cook pasta over medium heat until tender yet firm. Or, follow package directions.
  4. Drain and return pasta to pot. Mix butter, parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper into pasta until evenly combined.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
